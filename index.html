About the Artist

Hi, I'm Kristy Kate, artist and surface designer based in Melbourne, Australia. I create playful prints and patterns that celebrate the joy of childhood. My designs are inspired by the inner child, nature and all things cute. I love drawing our animal friends and exploring fun shapes and colours that speak to kids and the young at heart. There's nothing quite like a cute design to make your day shine! x Kristy Kate
